About

Abacus Computing Institute (AbacusCI) is an educational initiative based in Paramaribo, Suriname. It provides students and community members with opportunities to learn basic computer skills, use digital tools and develop problem-solving abilities through practical modules and teamwork. The goal is to help people feel more confident and capable using technology in daily life, school or work.

Team levels

Discovers

Students ages 11-12
They are beginning to interact with technology. The focus is on discovery, curiosity, and understanding of basic digital concepts through structured, playful experiences.

Explorers

Students ages 13 to 15
Begin to interact with computing more consciously. They experiment, connect ideas, and develop early technical fluency through guided practice.

Builders

Students 16 and older
They work with computing as a tool for creation and problem solving. Their learning is focused, applied and aligned with real-world productivity and development goals.
